Our Story

At Joseph seafoods we provide the world class fresh and frozen seafoods in India. we are operating from the state of tamilnadu for the past 25 years. We procure sea products from the coast of Tamil Nadu, kerela, pondicherry, Gujarat, Andhra Pradesh, Orissa and Karnataka. we supply our products to retail outlets, fish markets, hotels, banquet halls, restaurants and direct customers all over India.our team of professionals and 350+ dedicated experts work sincerely towards achieving good quality sea products with continuous growth and profitability.




Our Mission

To provide world class sea products without compromising on quality, variety that mesmarize taste buds, reliable excellent service, best competitive price with daily fresh fishes.




Our Vision

To be the leading fresh and frozen seafood exporter, processor and distributor with world class quality seafood that mesmerise the taste buds without compromising on quality that satisfy our customer.




Fish is from..

We take pride in purchasing our products directly from reputed suppliers/ fishermen. We own procurement center at main centers for daily fresh fishes. We have adequated insulated refrigerated vehicles to carry and maintain the freshness of our sea products.




Handling

We carefully handle our sea products freshly and hygienically with the best cold chain management. We ensure our customers receive the sea products as per their specification and at preferred timings.




Customer Satisfaction

JSF foods is committed to meet customers expectation and requirement with good quality variety of fresh sea products. Keeping pace with customer behaviour we JSF foods has value added product mix of ready to fry frozen products that includes crab lollipop, crab balls, crab cutlets, fish fingers, fish balls and fish cutlets.
